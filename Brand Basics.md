## Typography

### Primary Font

**Montserrat** is the only approved font for St Raphael Health.

### Approved Weights
- Light  
- Regular  
- Semibold  
- Bold  

### Usage
Montserrat must be used for:
- Headings
- Subheadings
- Body copy
- UI elements
- Marketing and internal materials
