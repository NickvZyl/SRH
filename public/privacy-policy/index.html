Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11 January 2026

St Raphael Health is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website. This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you interact with this website.

By using this website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

St Raphael Health may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through this website, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Organization or hospital name
  • City and state
  • Any information submitted through contact forms, inquiries, or correspondence

We do not collect patient health information, medical records, or protected health information (PHI) through this website.

How Information Is Used

Information submitted to St Raphael Health is used solely for legitimate business and communication purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries or requests for information
  • Communicating regarding advisory services or potential engagements
  • Maintaining internal records related to communications and inquiries
  • Improving the quality and clarity of our communications

St Raphael Health does not use personal information for mass marketing, advertising lists, or unsolicited outreach.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

St Raphael Health does not sell, rent, lease, or trade personal information collected through this website.

Information may be shared only under limited circumstances, including:

  • When necessary to respond to an inquiry or request
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating the website or managing communications, and who are required to maintain confidentiality
  • When required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests

Any information shared is limited to what is reasonably necessary for the applicable purpose.

Data Security

St Raphael Health maintains reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information submitted through this website.

While we take appropriate steps to protect information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. As such, St Raphael Health cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted through this website.

Cookies and Website Analytics

This website may use limited cookies or analytics tools to understand general website usage and improve functionality. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by the user.

You may adjust your browser settings to decline cookies; however, doing so may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Websites

This website may contain links to third-party websites for informational purposes. St Raphael Health is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of external websites.

We encourage visitors to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they choose to visit.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for use by professionals and adults. St Raphael Health does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

Updates to This Policy

St Raphael Health may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices, regulations, or operational needs. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
