Why we exist

St Raphael Health was formed when a group of hospital operators and M&A professionals partnered with a Houston-based hospital CEO to help save a 270+ bed hospital operating under Chapter 11.

That hospital was bleeding $85,000 per day. Within months, we stabilized operations, restored profitability, and positioned the facility for long-term success.

What started as one urgent intervention became a repeatable operating model. We realized the healthcare industry desperately needs operators who can execute—not just advise.
